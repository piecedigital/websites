Darryl Dixon

Aspiring Graphic and Web Designer

Though for the majority of my life I have had an interest in video production I was never too far from the visual and imaginative industry that is graphic design. Only recently (less than a year ago) have I discovered my interest in graphic design, an interest that has evolved deeply into a passion. I am mostly self-taught, having had taken a few computer arts classes in high school.

Outside of that I scour the internet for any resources to help me improve my craft and help me grow into the professional I would like to be. I've come a long way form where I began but my journey has yet to end.

I've also recently gotten web development at the start of October, '14. Since then I've made two websites (both are in my portfolio). I don't believe in perfection at all times but trying your best to achieve something functional and practical. Ergo I'm pretty proud of what I've done so far and will continue to improve on what I do and can do.
